## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Music Garden stores the following information **exclusively on your device**:

**Music piece data:**
- Names of music pieces
- Composer names (optional)
- Your notes on pieces
- Practice history (date, duration)
- Learning progress

**User settings:**
- Selected interface language
- Notification settings (enabled/disabled, reminder time)
- Sound and vibration settings
- Onboarding completion status

**Progress data:**
- Current practice streak
- Best practice streak
- Last practice date

This data **is not transmitted to our servers** and is stored exclusively locally on your device.

### 2.2. Subscription and Purchase Data

For processing subscriptions and in-app purchases, we use third-party services:

**RevenueCat:**
- Your subscription status
- Purchase history
- Customer ID (anonymous)
- Information about products you purchased

**Apple App Store:**
- All payments are processed directly by Apple
- We do not have access to your payment details (card number, CVV, etc.)

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1. Local Storage
All your personal data (music pieces, settings, progress) is stored **exclusively on your device** using Apple's SwiftData technology. We do not have access to this data.

### 6.2. No Cloud Synchronization
The App **does not synchronize** your data with cloud services. If you delete the App, all local data will be lost.

### 6.3. Security
Your data is protected by standard iOS security mechanisms, including device-level encryption.

### 6.4. Data Retention Period
- **Local data:** Stored on your device until you delete the App or specific items
- **Subscription data:** Retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription plus any legally required period

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your data:

### 7.1. Right to Access
You can view all your data directly in the App.

### 7.2. Right to Rectification
You can edit information about music pieces and settings at any time.

### 7.3. Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- You can delete individual music pieces in the App
- You can delete all data by removing the App from your device

### 7.4. Right to Data Portability
Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can access it through iOS backup mechanisms (iCloud or iTunes/Finder backup).

### 7.5. Right to Lodge a Complaint
If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority:
