## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is Music Garden?**
A: Music Garden is a practice tracker for musicians. It helps you organize your repertoire, track practice sessions, and maintain a daily practice streak.

**Q: Is Music Garden free?**
A: The core features are free. Music Garden Pro unlocks the full plant collection and additional features. You can subscribe weekly, monthly, or annually.

---

### Subscription & Purchases

**Q: How do I upgrade to Music Garden Pro?**
A: Go to **Settings → Upgrade to Pro** and choose a plan that works for you.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase after reinstalling the app?**
A: Go to **Settings → Music Garden Pro → Manage Subscription** and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. You can cancel at any time via:
**iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Music Garden**

**Q: I was charged but the app still shows the free version. What do I do?**
A: First, try restoring your purchases (see above). If the issue persists, please contact us at **alexdordiuk@gmail.com** with your order confirmation from Apple.

---

### Data & Privacy

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device. It is included in your iOS backups (iCloud or iTunes/Finder). We do not store your personal data on our servers.

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?**
A: All locally stored data will be permanently deleted. If you have iCloud backups enabled, you may be able to restore it by reinstalling the app and restoring from backup.

**Q: Does the app collect my personal data?**
A: We only collect anonymous analytics data (via Firebase) to improve the app. Your music pieces, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device only. See our [Privacy Policy](https://alexdordiuk.github.io/music-garden-legals/PRIVACY_POLICY_EN) for full details.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app crashes or isn't working correctly. What should I do?**
A: Please try the following steps:
1. Force-close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Make sure the app is updated to the latest version
4. If the issue persists, contact us at **alexdordiuk@gmail.com** with a description of the problem and your device model and iOS version.

**Q: Notifications aren't working.**
A: Check that notifications are enabled in:
- **Music Garden Settings → Notifications** (toggle must be on)
- **iPhone Settings → Music Garden → Notifications** (must be allowed)

**Q: The app is not available in my language.**
A: Music Garden currently supports English, Ukrainian, and Polish. You can change the language in **Settings → Language**. We plan to add more languages in future updates.
